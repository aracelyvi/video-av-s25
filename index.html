Favorite A24 Movies

This is a compilation of movie trailers that I have recently watched and enjoyed. PSA: I enjoy horror movies.

Heretic

Heretic is about faith and whether the main charcters will stick to their beliefs when put into a life-or-death situation.
It was creepy without being too scary. A good mix of psychological horror and thriller film.

Talk To Me

Talk To Me is a very interesting story about a game between friends going completely wrong. In Talk To Me, the main character
contacts the afterlife and uses that as a way to cope with the grief of her mom passing. This movie has become one of my favorite movies
in general because I really enjoy the ending. TW: TRAILER CONTAINS GRAPHIC CONTENT

The Florida Project

The Florida Project is a emotional movie that I think is very interesting, the movie follows the children's perspective. The troubles of
the children's situations are based on what we assumed from the kid's interactions. It is a very good movie but very sad as well.
